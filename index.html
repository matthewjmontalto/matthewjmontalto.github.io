About

Boston-based Software Engineer

I am a software engineer with an eclectic background that spans audio engineering, CRM administration, sales, and marketing. Following a realization that I was seeking out and energized by the most technical aspects of the work I performed, I committed myself to a career where I am able to fully explore my passion for building, creating, and problem-solving.

From each role I learned to enjoy collaboration with numerous personalities—how to harness creativity and leverage passionate energy. I was taught to value a well-crafted story and to know when listening with an open mind is most important. I view problems as opportunities and value disciplined optimization. As I look ahead to my career as a software engineer, I see a new opportunity to combine the talents I’ve honed these past years and apply them to developing software that is fun, powerful, useful, and valuable.
